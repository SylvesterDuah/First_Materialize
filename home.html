New Glenn, named after John Glenn, is a heavy-lift orbital launch vehicle in development by Blue Origin. Design work on the vehicle began in 2012. Illustrations of the vehicle, and the high-level specifications, were initially publicly unveiled in September 2016

Space Raptor Engine

The SpaceX Raptor is a highly reusable full-flow staged combustion, methane-fueled rocket engine manufactured by SpaceX. The engine is powered by cryogenic liquid methane and liquid oxygen, rather than the RP-1 kerosene and LOX used in SpaceX's prior Merlin and Kestrel rocket engines.
